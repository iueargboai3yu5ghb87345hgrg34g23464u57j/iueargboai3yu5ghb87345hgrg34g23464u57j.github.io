Rozwiązanie:

1. The first crime is described as crazy because the robbery suspect owned up to breaking into another place.

Fragment w tekście: "He said that he couldn't be guilty of robbing the jewellery store because on Saturday afternoon he was breaking into a school and he had a witness to prove it." - Powiedział, że nie może być winny napadu na sklep jubilerski, ponieważ w sobotę po południu włamywał się do szkoły i ma na to świadka.

2. The second crime is described as crazy because the criminal wrote his name on the wall.

Fragment w tekście: "The criminal wrote his name on a wall!" - Przestępca napisał swoje imię na ścianie!
