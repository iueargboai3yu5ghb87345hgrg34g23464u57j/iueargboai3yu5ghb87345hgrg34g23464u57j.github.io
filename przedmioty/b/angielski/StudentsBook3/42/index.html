Zadanie 3

Wyrażenia, które pojawiają się w nagraniu:

 - Can you do me a big favour?

 - Can you give me some advice? 

 - What do you think I should (take) ?

 - The first thing you should do is (check the weather forecast).

 - If I were you, I'd (pack lots of T-shirts and a couple of pullovers).

 - I think you should (check it in).

 - You really ought to (take some smart trousers).

 - You mustn't (forget to take them something).

 - The best thing would be to (get something at the airport).

 - It's a good idea to (write a list of thinks you might forget).

 - Good thinking!

 - Oh, I didn't think of that!

 - That's really helpful.
