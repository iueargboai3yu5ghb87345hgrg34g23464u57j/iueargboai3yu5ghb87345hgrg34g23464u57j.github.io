Zadanie 1

Uwaga! Zdania należy napisać używając czasu Present Perfect Continuous:

Tworzymy go w następujący sposób:

Twierdzenie: I/you/we/they + have + been + verb + -ing.

he/she/it + has + been + verb + -ing.

Przeczenie: I/you/we/they + have not (haven't) + been + verb + -ing.

he/she/it + has not (hasn't) + been + verb + -ing.

Pytania: Have + I/you/we/they + been + verb + -ing...?

Has + he/she/it + been + verb + -ing...?

 

Uwaga! "for" i "since to słowa charakterystyczne dla czasów typu present perfect. 

"for " używamy, gdy w zdaniu nacisk położony został na czas trwania czynności, np. for 5 years (od 5 lat).

"since" używamy z konkretnym momentem w przeszłości, np. since 2010 (od 2010 roku).

 

1. I've been studying art since 2009.

2. How long have you been wearing your glasses?

3. Have you been swimming all afternoon?

4. It has been raining for two hours now.

5. We haven't been watching the match.

6. My parents have been studying English for five years.

7. How long has Joanna been working in that cafe?
