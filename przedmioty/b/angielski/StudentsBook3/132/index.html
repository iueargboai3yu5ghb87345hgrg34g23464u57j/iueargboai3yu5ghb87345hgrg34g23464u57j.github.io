Zadanie 2

1. Would the explorers have survived ...

    had taken

2. hadn't warned ...

    would have made

3. had left ...

    would you have missed

4. hadn't taken ...

    wouldn't have found

5. would you have done ...

    hadn't let

*Przypomnienie:

Trzeci tryb warunkowy składa się z dwóch części - zdania z 'if' w czasie Past perfect, które opisuje wyobrażoną sytuację albo warunek niespełniony w przeszłości i zdania z would have i trzecią formą czasownika (past participle), które opisuje co by było, gdyby warunek jednak został spełniony.

Używamy tej konstrukcji kiedy mówimy o przeszłych wydarzeniach, które potoczyły się inaczej niż mogły i o tym jaki mogły przynieść skutek.

Kiedy pierwszym zdaniem jest zdanie z if, przed drugim zdaniem należy postawić przecinek.

Zadanie 3

1. They would have reached the North Pole if they hadn't run out of food.

2. If I hadn't been tired yesterday, I would have gone out with my friends.

3. It wouldn't have taken us ages to get to the airport if there hadn't been a lot of traffic.

4. If Ann had phoned me, I would have known about her problems with the equipment.

5. If he hadn't had an accident in the mountains, he wouldn't have gone to hospital.

6. If Gina had used a mosquito net, a mosquito wouldn't have bitten her and she wouldn't have become ill with malaria.

7. If Joe had worn warm clothes last weekend, he wouldn't have caught a cold.

8. We would have reached our destination if the weather conditions hadn't been so bad.
